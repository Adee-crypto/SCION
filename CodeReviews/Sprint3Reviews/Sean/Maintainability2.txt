There are many fields right now. Some of them seem like they should be combined into lists or even their own manager.

This is better for maintainability, such as for adding submenus (e.g. a specific SFX tab or Keybinding tab within Settings).

The casework in Update with the switch statement seems good, I don't imagine we'll need many more states than that.
