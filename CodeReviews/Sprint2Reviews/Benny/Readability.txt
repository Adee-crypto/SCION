The logic for handling axis-separated movement is straightforward and easy to follow.

The use of ref for velocity and movement parameters is clear in its intent to modify the calling state, though it requires the reader to be careful about side effects.

It might be more readable if split Y collision resolution into a standard if-else block to match the X-axis implementation.
