The grouping of computed properties (Center, Left, Right, Top, Bottom, Angle) in one block is clean and easy to navigate — these are exactly the values a reader would expect to find on a collider class.

The SetVelocityX/Y and SetPositionX/Y helper methods are a readable workaround for the immutability of Vector2, and their intent is immediately obvious.

The magic numbers 100f and -100f in KnockBack, and 0.97f in UpdatePlayerVelocity, would be more readable as named constants in Consts, both to convey their meaning and to make them easier to tune later.
